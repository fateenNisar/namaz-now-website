About Namaz-Now

Namaz-Now is a revolutionary, non-profit app designed to enhance your prayer experience by providing countdown timers to Jamaat times, Jamaat time notifications, and a Masjid locator. Seamlessly synchronizing with digital Meeqaat systems in Masaajid, Namaz-Now ensures accurate and timely updates.

For Masaajid without digital Meeqaat, committee members can manually update prayer times using the Namaz-Now admin app, making it accessible for all.
